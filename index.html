Front Stage & Backstage Alignment

Every customer interaction (front stage) depends on seamless backstage operations. Our methodology ensures both are designed in harmony for optimal service delivery.

Customer-Centric Approach

We put your customers at the heart of every service design decision, understanding their expectations and doubts at every journey stage.

Persona Development

We create detailed customer personas that go beyond demographics to capture motivations, pain points, and service expectations.

Journey Mapping

Visualizing every touchpoint helps identify moments of truth and opportunities for service improvement.

Emotional Insights

We uncover not just what customers do, but how they feel at each stage of their service experience.

Touchpoint Design Principles

Usability

Clarity

Brand Consistency

Service Blueprint & Manual

We provide comprehensive deliverables that align front-stage interactions with backstage operations to eliminate your functional barriers.

Service Blueprint Components

Customer Actions

Detailed steps customers take when interacting with your service.

Frontstage Actions

Visible employee actions and touchpoints that customers experience.

Backstage Actions

Support processes that enable frontstage service delivery.

Support Processes

Technology, systems and third-party services that enable delivery.

Service Manual Benefits

  • Provides a single source of truth for service delivery standards
  • Reduces service variability across locations and teams
  • Accelerates onboarding of new employees
  • Identifies opportunities for process automation
  • Aligns cross-functional teams around customer outcomes
